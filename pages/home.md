intro_detail: >
  We study the non-equilibrium dynamics of soft matter — colloidal suspensions,
  vitrimer nanocomposites, and battery electrode slurries — using
  <em>in situ</em> Rheo-SAXS-XPCS at the Advanced Photon Source. By tracking
  structure and motion from the nanoscale up, we build constitutive models and
  machine-learning tools that connect molecular rearrangements to macroscopic
  rheological transitions.
