Wei Chen, Ph.D.

Current Position

Chemist at Argonne National Laboratory with appointments in the Materials Science Division and Center for Molecular Engineering. Since 2016, maintains affiliation with the University of Chicago's Pritzker School of Molecular Engineering.

Research Focus

Developing sustainable smart composites through innovative synthesis, surface functionalization, advanced processing, and state-of-the-art characterization techniques.

Education

  • Postdoctoral Fellowship (2010–2012): Argonne Director's Fellow at Center for Nanoscale Materials, advised by Dr. Seth B. Darling
  • Ph.D. Polymer Science and Engineering (2005–2010): University of Massachusetts–Amherst, advised by Prof. Thomas P. Russell
  • M.S. Inorganic Chemistry (2000–2003): Jilin University, advised by Prof. Jie-Sheng Chen
  • B.S. Chemistry (1996–2000): Jilin University
