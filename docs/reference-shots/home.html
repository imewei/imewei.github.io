Welcome

MSD Soft Matter Lab

Adaptive, data-driven soft materials

Sustainable materials through responsive design

Located within Argonne National Laboratory’s Materials Science Division, the MSD Soft Matter Lab creates adaptive nanocomposites, hetero-charged interfaces, and bio-derived polymers that address energy and sustainability challenges.

What we study

  • Adaptive & dynamic nanocomposites – self-healing, recyclable systems driven by reversible bonding and smart fillers.
  • Non-equilibrium physics – operando scattering and rheology reveal how nanoscale motion shapes bulk response.
  • Sustainable biocomposites – bio-based architectures that extend lifetimes and reduce waste.
