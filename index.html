Chen Team

Materials Science Division, Argonne National Laboratory

Developing Sustainable Materials for a Better Future

Adaptive and Dynamic Nanocomposites

The team creates self-healing, recyclable materials using reversible bonds and nanofillers. These respond to physical and environmental stimuli for applications in biomedical, robotics, and energy sectors.

Non-Equilibrium Physics

Using advanced X-ray characterization (Rheo-SAXS-XPCS), researchers examine how nanostructures evolve under stress, leveraging the Advanced Photon Source upgrade.

Sustainable Biocomposites

The lab combines natural fibers with bio-based polymers using dynamic bonding to create environmentally-friendly alternatives to conventional plastics.
